Recipe-lasagna

Description

This is highly tasty and highly marinated or submerged with double rated cheese which is totally creamy and juicy while having a bite.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • corainder
  • pepper and salt
  • olive oil and much more

Steps

  1. Take three or four breads as needed
  2. Toast the bread with a butter on pan
  3. Add some pepper and salt to it
  4. Put some cheese on it
  5. place it on the oven for 20-30 minutes
  6. And voila, you got the dish
